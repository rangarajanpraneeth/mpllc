Petroleum products and services we provide
Michigan Petroleum LLC is a trusted provider of high-quality petroleum and petrochemical products, catering to a diverse range of industries and clients. With a commitment to excellence and reliability, we offer a comprehensive selection of products to meet the specific needs and requirements of our customers
Crude oil
Michigan Petroleum LLC trades in various grades of crude oil sourced from reputable suppliers around the world. Our crude oil products meet industry standards for quality and consistency, and we ensure reliable supply to support the energy needs of our clients.
Refined petroleum products
From gasoline and diesel fuel to jet fuel and heating oil, we offer a wide range of refined petroleum products to meet the demands of different sectors, including transportation, aviation, and industrial applications. Our products undergo strict quality control measures to ensure optimal performance and compliance with regulatory standards.
Specialty chemicals
Michigan Petroleum LLC also provides a variety of specialty chemicals used in the petrochemical industry for manufacturing processes, additives, and specialty applications. Our portfolio includes solvents, lubricants, additives, and other chemicals that meet industry specifications and contribute to the efficiency and performance of various industrial processes.
Petrochemical feedstocks
As a leading trader in the petrochemical industry, we supply essential feedstocks such as ethylene, propylene, and benzene that serve as building blocks for a wide range of chemical products. Our petrochemical feedstocks are sourced from reliable suppliers to ensure consistent quality and availability for our clients' manufacturing needs.
Lubricants and greases
Michigan Petroleum LLC offers a selection of high-quality lubricants, greases, and oils for automotive, industrial, and commercial applications. Our lubricant products are designed to enhance equipment performance, reduce friction, and extend the lifespan of machinery, providing cost-effective solutions for our customers.
