Our Logistics & Distribution Network
PACKING & LOGISTICS
As a provider of petroleum products, Michigan Petroleum LLC utilizes various modes of containers for the transportation and storage of these products. Here are some common modes of logistics used by Michigan Petroleum LLC for handling petroleum products:
local_shipping
Tank Trucks
Tank trucks equipped with specialized tanks are commonly used for transporting petroleum products over short to medium distances on roads and highways. These trucks are designed to safely carry liquid cargo such as gasoline, diesel, and other refined petroleum products.
inventory_2
Shipping Containers
Flexitanks are designed to fit inside standard 20-foot shipping containers, maximizing the storage capacity and allowing for easy loading and unloading of liquids. They are commonly used for transporting bulk liquids by sea, providing a secure and efficient means of international shipment.
view_in_ar
ISO Containers
ISO containers are standardized shipping containers that can be easily transported by road, rail, or sea. These containers are used for storing and transporting bulk quantities of petroleum products, providing a secure and efficient means of international transportation.
directions_railway
Rail Tank Cars
Rail tank cars are used for transporting large volumes of petroleum products by rail. These specialized tank cars are designed to safely carry liquids such as crude oil and refined products over long distances, offering a cost-effective mode of transportation.
sailing
Barges
Barges equipped with tanks are utilized for transporting petroleum products along rivers, canals, and coastal areas. These vessels provide a flexible and efficient mode of transportation for bulk liquids, offering access to waterways for delivery to various locations.
oil_barrel
Storage Tanks
Storage tanks are used for storing bulk quantities of petroleum products at terminals, refineries, and distribution centers. These tanks come in various sizes and configurations to accommodate different types of products, providing a safe and reliable storage solution for petroleum products.
package
Drums and Containers
Smaller containers such as drums and intermediate bulk containers (IBCs) are used for storing and transporting smaller quantities of petroleum products, offering a convenient and portable solution for handling various types of liquids.
By utilizing a combination of these containers and modes of transportation, Michigan Petroleum LLC is able to efficiently handle, store, and transport petroleum products to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Our focus on safety, reliability, and efficiency in container management ensures the secure and timely delivery of petroleum products to various locations, supporting the operational requirements of our customers in the oil trading industry.
